Know more about us

London Institute of English is a team of qualified English-language specialists and the author of an advanced Internet platform.

Thanks to their close cooperation we have created an Internet-based language course, offering the quality of a conventional language school while allowing You to study at any place and any time.

The aim of creating the platform was to make it easier for people who do not have the time to attend traditional language schools to have access to learning another language. We are constantly working on developing the platform so that it features an ever richer range of interesting teaching materials and innovative Internet solutions.

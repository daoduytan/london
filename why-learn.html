Why learn with us

Work, travel, using the Intertet, these are just a few situations where it is useful to be able to use English. It's not a question of whether it is worth learning a language, but of how to learn it so that You can reap the benefits most effectively, even if You have little time on Your hands.

At the London Institute of English we know how it can be done!

The special learning programme prepared with You in mind

Conversations, vocabulary, simmulated dialogues, grammar – everything is presented in an attractive way, to make sure You not only make Your language skills better but also keep them that way. The exam which is taken at the beginning of the course helps place You at the ideal level for Your needs and abilities.

Learn English? It's simple!

Thanks to the London Institute of English courses, You will be convinced that learning English can in fact be simple. The special language platform makes it possible for You to expand Your knowledge of English and also develop specific language skills. Once any language issue is dealt with, it is strenthened at further levels of the course. You alone decide the pace and the duration of Your studies.

Receive a free London Institute of English Certificate

When You complete a London Institute of English course, You will be allowed to take a free examination, as a result of which we will present You with a certificate. A London School of English certificate will prove useful not only in everyday life but also in Your work.
